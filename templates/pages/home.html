GroupBot A group simplification platform.

Are you a part of a school club, community group, or other group of people trying to do something together?

GroupBot is designed to make coordinating and documenting each of your groups totally painless. No more hunting through old email threads, no more outdated records, no more scrambling to keep things organized.

Sound good to you? Then make an account now!

GroupBot puts all of the minutiae for each group in one place. Whether it's a study group, a living group, or anything in between, GroupBot can make management simpler.

When you make a Group, you can add Members. Those Members can have Roles (like President, Secretary, or Brother).

Roles simplify running your group by putting all of the related information in one place that stays the same when the person performing the Role changes. After the next President is elected, they step into the Role and use or build on the documentation created by the Member before them.

Members can create and assign each other Tasks. They can also create Events, as well as invite people, RSVP, and take attendance.
