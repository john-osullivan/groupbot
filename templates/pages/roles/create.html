Oooh, this is a biggie! Time to make this group some Roles.

Roles are used to make it easier to manage the different activities in your Group, as well as collect all the information related to that Role in one place. Each Role has its own page where all of its information is collected. The information could be anything from a description of the President's duties to a Treasurer's summary of Group finances.

What makes Roles really convenient, however, is using them to "group" people together (ba dum pshh). Since many Members can have one Role and each Member can also have many Roles, you can use them to easily manage your group. For example, you could:

  • Assign a Task to every Member with a given Role
  • Invite every Member with some Role to an Event
  • [IN DEVELOPMENT] Manage Permissions by saying which Roles can see which content

With all that said, let's get started!
